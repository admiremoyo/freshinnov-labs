Features

Everything Your School
Needs to Run Smoothly

👤

Student Registration & Profiles

Register students with full profiles including personal details, parent contacts, class assignment, and admission date. Search and filter students instantly.

💰

Fee Management & Payment Tracking

Record fee payments, track balances, and see which students are outstanding at a glance. Generate fee statements and payment receipts automatically.

📊

Generate Reports & Transcripts

Generate student report cards, end-of-term reports, and academic transcripts automatically. Professional, branded documents ready to print or share.

📅

Class Scheduling & Timetables

Create and manage class timetables easily. Assign teachers to classes, manage subject allocations, and prevent scheduling conflicts automatically.

✅

Attendance Tracking

Record daily attendance for each class. View attendance history for any student, generate attendance reports, and identify students who need follow-up.

Teacher & Staff Management

Manage teacher profiles, subject assignments, and class allocations. Track staff information and generate teacher-specific reports with ease.
