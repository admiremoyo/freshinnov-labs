Features

Track Every Dollar
In & Out

💵

Record Sales Instantly

Tap to add a sale in seconds. Name the category, enter the amount, and it's saved. No complicated forms, no training needed.

📉

Track All Expenses

Record rent, restocking, transport, wages — any expense. The app automatically calculates your net profit after deducting all expenses.

📶

Works Without Internet

Fully offline — no SIM card or WiFi needed. Perfect for market stalls, rural shops, and anywhere connectivity is unreliable.

📊

Daily, Weekly & Monthly Reports

View your profit for today, this week, or this month with one tap. Spot your best days, worst days, and see trends over time.

📂

Multiple Categories

Organise your sales and expenses by category — groceries, electronics, clothing, beverages — whatever suits your business.

🔒

Secure & Private

Your financial data stays on your phone. No cloud uploads, no accounts required to get started. Your data is yours.
